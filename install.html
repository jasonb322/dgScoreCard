Install DG ScoreCard

You can install DG ScoreCard as a progressive web app (PWA) on your phone. This means that even though the app is hosted on the web, you can experience it on your phone as if it was a native app. Here's how:

Apple Devices

  1. Open Safari and navigate to dgscorecard.com
  2. Tap the share icon at the bottom of the screen (the square with the arrow pointing out of it)
  3. Scroll down to choose "Add to Home Screen" from the menu.

Android Devices

  1. Open Chrome and navigate to dgscorecard.com
  2. Tap install and follow the on screen instructions.
